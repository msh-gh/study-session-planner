Purpose: To assist learners in organizing study schedules, breaking down topics, and generating personalized study plans.

**How It Works**
Input: You provide details like subject, topics, available time, and goals.

AI Output: The app generates a structured study plan with:
Suggested time slots.
Topic breakdowns.
Revision and practice intervals.
Productivity tips (like Pomodoro or active recall).
Customization: You can adjust based on your pace, exam deadlines, or preferred learning style.

**Benefits**
Personalized Planning: Tailors study sessions to your available time and subject complexity.
Efficiency: Helps avoid procrastination by giving a clear roadmap.
Flexibility: Can be reused for different subjects or exams.
AI-Powered: Uses generative AI to adapt plans dynamically, unlike static timetables.

**Example Use Case**
Suppose you have 3 hours to prepare for a Data Structures exam:
Input: “Data Structures, focus on Trees and Graphs, 3 hours available.”

Output:
30 min → Quick review of Trees.
45 min → Practice problems on BST.
30 min → Graph theory concepts.
45 min → Solve graph problems.
30 min → Recap + flashcards.

**Why It’s Useful**
Study Session Planner Pro is especially helpful for students like you (final-year CSE, preparing for interviews and exams) because it can:
Break down algorithm topics into manageable chunks.
Suggest revision cycles before exams.
Help balance coding practice vs theory review.
